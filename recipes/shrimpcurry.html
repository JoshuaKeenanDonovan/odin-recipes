Coconut Shrimp Curry

A tangy shrimp dish with a creamy sauce with some kick that is best served over rice

Indgredients

Shrimp Marinade

  • Shrimp --Extra large, raw, deshellled and deveined
  • Seasoning --Salt and pepper
  • Spices --Cayenne pepper for the marinade
  • Lemon Juice --Having an acidic base helps penetrate the meat

Sauce

  • Coconut oil --Using this Ensures that coconut will be present in every note of the dish
  • Onion --An onion that cooks down well like a white or yellow
  • Aromatics --Fresh ginger and garlic
  • Spices --Ground coriander, curry poweder and turmeric
  • Tomatoes --One large can of diced tomatoes
  • Coconut milk --Rich, delicious and the creamy component of the curry
  • Cilantro --Chopped up nice and fine for garnish. If you don't like cilantro use parsley instead
